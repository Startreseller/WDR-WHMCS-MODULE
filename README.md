# WHMCS-module-for-Startreseller
StartReseller WHMCS Domain Registrar Module Allow your customers to register or transfer global domain TLDs directly through your website. You can offer a wide range of domain extensions, enabling customers to easily search, register, and transfer domains online.

With automated provisioning, domain registration, transfer, and renewal processes are completed instantly after successful payment for the selected TLD, ensuring a seamless and efficient experience for your customers.
